# Translatar 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**最后更新日期 / Last Updated: 2026-02-13**

## 中文版

### 引言

Translatar（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护您的个人隐私。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本隐私政策的条款。

### 1. 信息收集

**1.1 音频数据**

本应用的核心功能是实时语音翻译。为实现此功能，我们需要通过设备麦克风（包括AirPods麦克风）捕获音频数据。

- **在线模式**：音频数据将通过加密连接发送至OpenAI API进行翻译处理。翻译完成后，音频数据不会被永久存储。
- **离线模式**：音频数据完全在您的设备上处理，不会传输到任何外部服务器。

**1.2 API密钥**

您提供的OpenAI API密钥仅存储在您的本地设备上（通过iOS Keychain或UserDefaults），不会上传至我们的服务器或任何第三方。

**1.3 翻译历史**

翻译历史记录仅存储在您的本地设备上，不会同步到云端。您可以随时在应用内清除所有历史记录。

**1.4 订阅信息**

订阅购买通过Apple App Store处理。我们不会直接收集或存储您的支付信息。订阅状态通过Apple StoreKit 2 API进行验证。

### 2. 信息使用

我们收集的信息仅用于以下目的：
- 提供实时语音翻译服务
- 显示翻译历史记录
- 验证订阅状态以解锁高级功能
- 改善应用性能和用户体验

### 3. 信息共享

我们不会出售、交易或以其他方式向第三方转让您的个人信息。以下情况除外：
- **OpenAI API**：在线翻译模式下，音频数据将发送至OpenAI进行处理。请参阅 [OpenAI隐私政策](https://openai.com/privacy)。
- **Apple**：订阅购买通过Apple处理。请参阅 [Apple隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/privacy)。

### 4. 数据安全

我们采取以下措施保护您的数据安全：
- 所有网络传输均使用TLS/SSL加密
- API密钥使用iOS安全存储机制
- 离线模式下数据完全不离开设备
- 不在服务器端存储任何用户音频数据

### 5. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。

### 6. 您的权利

您有权：
- 随时删除应用内的所有翻译历史
- 撤销麦克风和语音识别权限
- 删除应用以清除所有本地数据
- 联系我们要求删除任何相关数据

### 7. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在应用内发布，并更新"最后更新日期"。

### 8. 联系我们

如有任何隐私相关问题，请联系：
- 邮箱：support@translatar.app

## English Version

### Introduction

Translatar ("the App") respects and protects your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

### 1. Information Collection

**1.1 Audio Data**

The core function of the App is real-time voice translation. To provide this service, we need to capture audio data through your device's microphone (including AirPods microphone).

- **Online Mode**: Audio data is sent to OpenAI API via encrypted connection for translation processing. Audio data is not permanently stored after translation.
- **Offline Mode**: Audio data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

**1.2 API Keys**

Your OpenAI API key is stored only on your local device (via iOS Keychain or UserDefaults) and is never uploaded to our servers or any third party.

**1.3 Translation History**

Translation history is stored only on your local device and is not synced to the cloud. You can clear all history at any time within the App.

**1.4 Subscription Information**

Subscription purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment information. Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit 2 API.

### 2. Use of Information

Information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:
- Providing real-time voice translation services
- Displaying translation history
- Verifying subscription status to unlock premium features
- Improving app performance and user experience

### 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:
- **OpenAI API**: In online translation mode, audio data is sent to OpenAI for processing. See [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).
- **Apple**: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. See [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy).

### 4. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:
- All network transmissions use TLS/SSL encryption
- API keys use iOS secure storage mechanisms
- In offline mode, data never leaves the device
- No user audio data is stored on our servers

### 5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all translation history within the App at any time
- Revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions
- Delete the App to clear all local data
- Contact us to request deletion of any related data

### 7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted within the App with an updated "Last Updated" date.

### 8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact:
- Email: support@translatar.app
