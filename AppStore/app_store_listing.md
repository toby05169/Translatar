## 应用描述（简体中文）

戴上AirPods，世界上的每一种语言都变成了你的母语。

Translatar不是又一个翻译工具——它是一种全新的语言体验。基于最先进的AI大模型，Translatar让你在任何场景下都能自然地"听懂"外语，就像你突然掌握了一门新语言。

**为什么选择Translatar？**

传统翻译软件需要你掏出手机、点击按钮、等待结果。Translatar完全不同：戴上AirPods，选好语言，轻轻一点，翻译就开始了。对方说的每一句话，都会以自然流畅的语音实时传入你的耳朵，同时屏幕上显示双语字幕。

**两种模式，覆盖所有场景**

对话模式：适合面对面交流。AI自动识别对话轮次，你说一句、对方说一句，翻译无缝衔接。

沉浸模式：专为机场、车站、会议等场景设计。持续监听周围所有语音，自动翻译广播通知。登机口变更、航班延误、列车到站——再也不会错过任何重要信息。

**核心技术优势**

AI智能降噪：即使在嘈杂的候机大厅或繁忙的街道，也能精准捕捉人声，过滤环境噪音。

离线翻译：没有网络也不怕。基于Apple原生AI引擎，离线模式下依然可以进行基础翻译，旅途中网络不稳定时自动无缝切换。

毫秒级响应：基于OpenAI最新的Realtime API，语音到语音直接翻译，延迟低至数百毫秒，对话不再有尴尬的等待。

**支持10种语言**

## App Description (English)

Put on your AirPods, and every language in the world becomes your native tongue.

Translatar isn't just another translation tool — it's an entirely new language experience. Powered by the most advanced AI models, Translatar lets you naturally "understand" foreign languages in any situation, as if you suddenly mastered a new language.

**Why Translatar?**

Traditional translation apps require you to pull out your phone, tap buttons, and wait for results. Translatar is completely different: put on your AirPods, select your languages, tap once, and translation begins. Every word spoken by others flows into your ears as natural, fluent speech in real-time, with bilingual subtitles on screen.

**Two Modes for Every Scenario**

Conversation Mode: Perfect for face-to-face communication. AI automatically detects conversation turns for seamless back-and-forth translation.

Immersive Mode: Designed for airports, train stations, and conferences. Continuously monitors all surrounding speech and automatically translates announcements. Gate changes, flight delays, train arrivals — never miss critical information again.

**Core Technology**

AI Noise Suppression: Precisely captures human voice even in noisy airport terminals or busy streets, filtering out environmental noise.

Offline Translation: No internet? No problem. Powered by Apple's native AI engine, basic translation works offline with automatic seamless switching when network is unstable.

Sub-second Response: Built on OpenAI's latest Realtime API for direct speech-to-speech translation with latency as low as hundreds of milliseconds.

**10 Languages Supported**

Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian — covering major travel destinations worldwide.

## 审核备注（Review Notes）

本应用需要以下权限：

麦克风权限：用于捕获语音进行实时翻译。这是应用的核心功能，没有麦克风权限应用无法工作。

语音识别权限：用于离线模式下的设备端语音识别（Apple SFSpeechRecognizer）。

后台音频权限：沉浸模式需要在后台持续监听环境音并进行翻译（如机场广播翻译）。

测试说明：在线翻译功能需要有效的OpenAI API密钥。审核时可使用以下测试方式：（1）在设置页面输入有效的API密钥；（2）或使用离线模式进行测试，离线模式不需要API密钥。
